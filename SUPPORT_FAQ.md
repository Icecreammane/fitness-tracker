
### "How do I set my macro goals?"

```
Hi [Name],

To set your daily macro goals:

1. Dashboard ‚Üí Settings (gear icon)
2. Enter:
   - Target weight
   - Daily calories
   - Daily protein (g)
   - Daily carbs (g)
   - Daily fat (g)
3. Save

Your dashboard will now show progress bars for each macro based on your goals!

Not sure what your macros should be? I recommend:
- Protein: 0.8-1g per lb of body weight
- Fat: 0.3-0.4g per lb of body weight
- Carbs: Fill the rest of your calories

Best,
Ross
```
